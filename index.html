Precision Korean Styling, Trusted by Atlanta

JD Beauty Salon brings authentic Korean hair artistry to Duluth, Georgia. From our first chair to our second location, we've built a reputation on precision cutting, expert color work, and a level of personal care that turns first-time guests into regulars.

English-Friendly— Full consultations and service for non-Korean speaking clients.
Master Stylists— Trained in Korean and Western cutting and coloring techniques.
